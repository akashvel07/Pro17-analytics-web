Cyber Security

We are dedicated to providing top-notch cybersecurity solutions to safeguard your digital assets. Our approach combines advanced technologies and proactive strategies to ensure comprehensive protection against cyber threats.

APPLICATION SECURITY

We are data analytic solution provider that helps organizations to develop their data and analytics estate.

At Pro17Analytics, Application Security involves implementing robust measures to protect applications from threats and vulnerabilities throughout their lifecycle, ensuring the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of data.

  • Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT)
  • Secure Code Review
  • Web Application Firewall (WAF)
  • Static & Dynamic Application Security Testing (SAST/DAST)
  • Secure Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC)
NETWORK SECURITY

Network Security involves implementing measures to protect a network.

At Pro17Analytics, Network Security involves implementing advanced measures to protect your network from unauthorized access, threats, and vulnerabilities, ensuring the safety and integrity of your data and systems.

  • Firewall Configuration & Management
  • Intrusion Detection & Prevention Systems (IDS/IPS)
  • Network Segmentation & Micro-Segmentation
  • Network Traffic Analysis & Monitoring
  • DDoS Protection
CLOUD SECURITY

Cloud Security involves implementing measures to protect cloud-based infrastructure.

We provide IAM for strong authentication and authorization, Cloud Encryption to protect data, Cloud Vulnerability Management to identify and fix vulnerabilities, Cloud Security Monitoring & Incident Response for threat detection and response, and Cloud Backup & Disaster Recovery to ensure data protection and business continuity.

  • Identity & Access Management (IAM)
  • Cloud Encryption
  • Cloud Vulnerability Management
  • Cloud Security Monitoring & Incident Response
  • Cloud Backup & Disaster Recovery
GOVERNANCE, RISK, AND COMPLIANCE (GRC)

Cloud Security involves implementing measures to protect cloud-based infrastructure.

Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) involves aligning IT activities with business goals, managing risks effectively, and ensuring adherence to laws and regulations to maintain operational integrity and accountability.

  • Risk Assessment & Management
  • Regulatory Compliance Internal Audits
  • Policy Management & Enforcement
  • Third-Party Risk Management
