This Cookie Policy explains how Pro17 Analytics uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or mobile device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, improve the user browsing experience, and provide information to site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to enhance functionality, analyze site performance, and assist with our marketing efforts. Specifically, we use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the basic operation of our site, such as maintaining security and allowing page navigation.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: Helping us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting information anonymously (e.g., page views, session length).
  • Functionality Cookies: Remembering your preferences and customization choices to deliver a more tailored experience.

3. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our first-party cookies, we may also work with third-party service providers (such as Google Analytics) who place cookies on your device to gather metrics about your site visits and traffic sources.

4. Managing and Disabling Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences through their settings menu. You can configure your browser to reject all cookies, accept only certain cookies, or delete cookies that have already been saved. Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect the usability of our website.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technologies, regulatory requirements, or our operational practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
