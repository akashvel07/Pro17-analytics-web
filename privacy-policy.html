Home \ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 8, 2026

At Pro17 Analytics, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you contact us, request consulting services, or subscribe to our communications. This information may include:

  • Contact details (name, email address, phone number).
  • Business information (company name, job title, industry).
  • Message details or files you submit via our contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for various purposes, including:

  • To deliver and improve our consulting, data analytics, and cloud engineering services.
  • To respond to your inquiries and offer client support.
  • To send newsletters, updates, and promotional materials (you can opt-out at any time).
  • To monitor and analyze usage trends to optimize website functionality.

3. Data Protection and Security

We implement robust physical, technical, and administrative security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no transmission of data over the internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to analyze web traffic and customize your browsing experience. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings, though this may disable certain features on our site.

5. Third-Party Sharing

Pro17 Analytics does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our platform, provided they agree to maintain strict confidentiality.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (such as under GDPR or CCPA guidelines), you may have rights to access, correct, restrict, or request the deletion of your personal data held by us. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.
